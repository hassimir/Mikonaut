This one is strange. I got these weird black streaks near the Owl Nebula and they exist on some exposured but not all of them, ruling out the sensor being dirty.<br>
My running guess is the sensor was picking up some refraction from a nearby lightsource because as the nebula got closer to the horizon it picked up the marks but near the zenith it did not appear.<br>
